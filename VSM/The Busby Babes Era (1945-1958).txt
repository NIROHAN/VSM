Document 3: The Busby Babes Era (1945-1958)
Under Sir Matt Busby, Manchester United blossomed into a formidable force. The youthful team, known as the "Busby Babes," won three league titles in five years. Tragically, this era was cut short when eight players lost their lives in the Munich air disaster in 1958, profoundly impacting the club and its supporters.
